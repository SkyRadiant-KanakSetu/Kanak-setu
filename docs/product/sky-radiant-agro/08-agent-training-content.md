# Agent Training Content (PDF-Ready)

## 1. Program Purpose

Train Sky Radiant field, procurement, quality, and trading teams to use AI recommendations in daily operations.

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities

- Procurement Agent: executes buy plan and verifies mandi opportunities.
- Quality Agent: enforces grade checklist and rejection criteria.
- Storage Agent: tracks shelf-life and environment compliance.
- Trading Agent: executes sell decisions and margin protection.

## 4. Daily Operating SOP

1. Open dashboard at 6:00 AM.
2. Review top BUY opportunities and risk flags.
3. Lock procurement targets by 7:30 AM.
4. Run quality checks at source.
5. Confirm storage and dispatch plan.
6. Record execution outcomes by EOD.

## 5. Exception Handling

- If confidence is low, escalate to manager.
- If weather risk is high, reduce quantity and shorten holding period.
- If transport disruption risk rises, reroute or delay procurement.

## 6. KPI Scorecard

- Forecast vs realized margin
- Rejection rate by commodity
- Spoilage percentage
- On-time dispatch rate
- Recommendation adherence rate
