# Investor Pitch Outline

## Slide 1: Title

Sky Radiant Agro  
Agri-Commodity Intelligence and Procurement Platform

## Slide 2: Problem

- Agri trade decisions are fragmented and intuition-driven.
- Price volatility + weather uncertainty reduce margins.
- Poor storage and logistics decisions increase spoilage.

## Slide 3: Solution

Multi-agent intelligence platform delivering:
- procurement timing
- buy/sell/hold decisions
- storage and quality SOPs
- logistics optimization

## Slide 4: Product

- Live mandi price intelligence
- Weather-adjusted risk scoring
- Margin forecast engine
- Operational dashboard and audit logs

## Slide 5: Data Advantage

- Market + weather + logistics + internal outcomes in one loop
- Recommendation traceability and accuracy tracking
- Continuous model recalibration using realized outcomes

## Slide 6: Business Model

- SaaS subscription (per seat + per market)
- Enterprise intelligence packages
- Advisory and implementation services

## Slide 7: GTM

- Start with North India high-volume commodities
- Channel partnerships with mandis, storage operators, and buyers
- Content-led inbound via commodity intelligence reports

## Slide 8: Traction Metrics (Template)

- monthly recommendations generated
- recommendation adoption rate
- margin uplift percentage
- spoilage reduction percentage
- recurring revenue

## Slide 9: Moat

- Proprietary execution-outcome dataset
- Workflow lock-in across procurement to dispatch
- Domain-tuned intelligence and SOP integration

## Slide 10: Ask

- Capital for product, data integrations, and expansion
- Strategic introductions to institutional buyers and logistics partners
- Advisory support for national scale-out
